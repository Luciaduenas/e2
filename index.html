Nature is a gift

Love Peonies is a zero-waste, plastic-free, vegan, and cruelty-free skincare brand with a deep love for the planet.

Our Products Learn more

Love Peonies is the future of sustainable and ethical self-care. We harness the power of raw, organic, plant-based ingredients to create luxury skincare, while offering a plastic free alternative to mainstream brands. We are a small, Australia based, women led team. Our owner and founder Lucile Wild is a practicing herbalist and uses her knowledge and expertise to guide all parts of our production process.

Zero waste skincare

Love Peonies is taking action for a more sustainable future.

Verified Sustainability

Love Peonies has met ethy’s standards for verified sustainability claims. By achieving ethy certification, helping consumers make informed decisions.

Small batched

We handmake our organic skincare in small batches, using fresh and local ingredients. we ensure all of our ingredients are sourced ethically.

Refill system

We operate on a circular refill system, where you can return your used jars and bottles to us to be sterilised and refilled, for a discount on your next purchase.
